Frequently Asked Questions?

Is MMS only valuable for students?

MMS is not just a product for health students. It also serves health practitioners as well. While health students use it for their everyday training, health practitioners use it for rehearsing medical procedures before commencing on a live patient

What are the advantages why someone should really consider MMS?

Virtual medial simulation, like MMS, has proven to be 83% more cost-effective than the regular high-fidelity mannequine-based simulations. This approach increases clinical performance by 2505, 4 times faster and learners and practitioners are 4 times more focused as well. Win-win to all involved, inclding the patients.
